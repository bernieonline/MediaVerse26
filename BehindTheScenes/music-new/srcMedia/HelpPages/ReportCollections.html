Welcome to the MediaManager Reports Section

Here you can view the details about the collections you have recorded

Here's how it works:

  • The collections summary is initially displayed as a Bar Chart
  • The Label to the left providing the collection Name and the number to the Right, the file count
  • Use the Filter Lists to select a Device and/or Category
  • Use the Radio Buttons to switch between Master and Media Collections
  • The Chart button refreshes the display
  • Simply click on a row and its details will be displayed in a table on the Right
  • Click on any Cell and its contents will be available on the clipboard
  • You could now, select the database search option from the Menu and paste your selection into the Text box. Click Search
  • You will now see a list of all records containing that text in the File_Path field
