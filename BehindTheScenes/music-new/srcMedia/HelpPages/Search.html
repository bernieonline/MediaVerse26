Welcome to the MediaManager Database Search Function

It's simple to use, enter text and click the button

Here's how it works:

  • All of the Master and Media records are store in two database tables, one for Master Collections and One for Media Collections
  • The search takes place on the file_path field.
  • Typically this field will hold the name of the item, the file type and in the case of iTunes Libraries Album Names and Artists
  • Videos may be stored in a grouping folder as may Images
  • Enter the text that you want to search for e.g. 'Dancing in the Dark' and you will get a display of all records with that text in the file path
  • Click any Cell in the display and it's contents will be copied, ready for pasting elsewhere
