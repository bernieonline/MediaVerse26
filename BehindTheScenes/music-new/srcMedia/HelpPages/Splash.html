Welcome to the MediaManager Tips Section

The software that puts you back in charge of your Media Files.

Here's how it works:

  • Our default Collections are Video, Music and Image.
  • But you can make your own with associated file extensions.
  • The Creating Option lets you choose a starting folder and the software will drill down and record details of each media file found. The record will have a collection name and other data
  • You may be best starting with your master files creation, you can then compare them with your other media collections and keep track of both
  • Once done you can decide what to do with your Non Master collections

You will need to install MySql from the packages MySqlDump and set up connection details in the env file.
